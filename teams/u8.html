About the Team

The Under-8 team is our youngest group, where children begin their soccer journey in a fun, nurturing environment. Players learn basic motor skills, coordination, and the joy of playing as part of a team while developing a love for the beautiful game.

Training Focus

  • Introduction to ball handling and basic dribbling
  • Fundamental movement and coordination
  • Basic concepts of passing and teamwork
  • Fun games and activities to develop soccer skills
  • Building confidence and love for the game

Season Highlights

2025 Season

Record: Focus on development, not competition

Our U8 team had a wonderful first season with excellent attendance and enthusiasm. The players showed remarkable improvement in their basic skills and made great friendships. At this age, we emphasize fun and development over competitive results.
