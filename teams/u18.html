About the Team

The Under-18 team represents the pinnacle of development at Africa Bora Soccer Academy. These players train with the intensity and professionalism needed to compete at higher levels. Many alumni have gone on to play collegiate soccer, and several have signed with professional clubs.

Training Focus

  • High-level technical refinement
  • Advanced tactical systems and flexibility
  • Elite physical conditioning
  • Mental toughness and game management
  • Leadership and mentorship of younger players
  • Preparation for next-level opportunities

Season Highlights

2025 Season

Record: 16 wins, 3 draws, 2 losses

Achievement: Regional Champions & National Tournament Semi-Finalists

An exceptional season saw the U18 team dominate regional competition and advance to the national semi-finals. Five players received scholarship offers from universities, and two signed professional contracts. The team's disciplined play and never-quit attitude inspired the entire academy.
