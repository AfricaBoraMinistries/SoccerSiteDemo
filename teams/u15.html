About the Team

The Under-15 team represents a critical development stage where players refine their technical skills and deepen their tactical understanding of the game. This team regularly competes in regional tournaments and has earned a reputation for disciplined, team-oriented play.

Training Focus

  • Advanced ball control and first touch
  • Tactical positioning and formations
  • Set piece execution (corners, free kicks)
  • Physical conditioning and fitness
  • Game intelligence and decision-making
  • Leadership development

Season Highlights

2025 Season

Record: 12 wins, 4 draws, 3 losses

Achievement: Champions - District Youth Cup

The U15 team captured the District Youth Cup championship with a thrilling 2-1 victory in the final. The team's technical improvement and tactical discipline were on full display throughout the tournament. Three players were selected for regional all-star team.
