About the Team

The Under-13 team represents an important development stage where players begin to refine their technical skills and develop tactical understanding. This team competes in local tournaments and emphasizes disciplined, team-oriented play.

Training Focus

  • Improved ball control and first touch
  • Understanding positioning and formations
  • Introduction to set piece execution
  • Building fitness and conditioning
  • Decision-making under pressure
  • Team communication skills

Season Highlights

2025 Season

Record: 10 wins, 3 draws, 4 losses

Achievement: Quarter-Finalists - District Youth Cup

The U13 team had a strong season with notable improvement in tactical awareness and teamwork. Several players were recognized for their sportsmanship and dedication, earning spots on the regional development squad.
