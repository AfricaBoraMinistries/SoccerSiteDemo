About the Team

The Under-11 team focuses on building a strong foundation in soccer fundamentals while making the game fun and engaging for young players. This is where players develop their core skills and understanding of the game.

Training Focus

  • Basic ball control and dribbling
  • Passing accuracy and receiving
  • Introduction to positioning
  • Teamwork and communication
  • Sportsmanship and fair play

Season Highlights

2025 Season

Record: 8 wins, 3 draws, 2 losses

Achievement: Runner-up in Regional Youth League

Our U11 team had an outstanding season, showing tremendous improvement in skills and teamwork. The team's dedication to training and positive attitude made them one of the most improved teams in the league.
