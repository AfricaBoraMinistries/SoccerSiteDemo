Biography

Sarah Omondi brings both competitive experience and coaching expertise to Africa Bora Soccer Academy. A former collegiate standout, she has dedicated her post-playing career to developing the next generation of talented players.

Sarah joined the academy in 2020 and has quickly established herself as one of the region's top youth coaches. Her tactical knowledge, combined with her ability to connect with teenage players, has transformed the U15 program into a championship-caliber team.

Coaching Experience

  • 2020-Present: U15 Head Coach, Africa Bora Soccer Academy
  • 2019-2020: Assistant Coach, Women's University Team
  • 2018-2019: Youth Coach, Elite Sports Academy

Playing Background

Sarah was a four-year starter at the University of Nairobi, where she played central midfield and earned All-Conference honors three times. She captained her team to two conference championships and was named to the All-Academic team for her excellence both on the field and in the classroom.

After university, she played semi-professionally for two seasons before transitioning to coaching, bringing valuable high-level playing experience to her coaching approach.

Certifications & Education

  • CAF B Coaching License (2021)
  • Sports Psychology Certificate (2022)
  • Advanced Tactical Analysis Course (2023)
  • First Aid & CPR Certified (2025)
  • Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology, University of Nairobi (2017)

Coaching Philosophy

"This age group is where players really start to understand the tactical side of the game. My goal is to develop smart, versatile players who can think critically and adapt to different game situations. But just as important is helping them develop confidence and leadership skills that will serve them throughout their lives."

Sarah believes in challenging her players while providing the support they need to succeed. She emphasizes the importance of mental toughness, teamwork, and continuous improvement. Her training sessions are demanding but always purposeful, with clear objectives that help players understand their development.

Achievements

  • Led U15 team to District Youth Cup Championship (2025)
  • Coached three players selected for regional all-star team (2025)
  • Developed two players who received scholarship offers to attend sports academies
  • Named "Coach of the Tournament" at Regional Youth Championships (2024)

Community Involvement

Beyond coaching, Sarah is passionate about promoting girls' participation in soccer. She organizes monthly girls-only clinics and serves as a mentor for young female players throughout the community. She also volunteers with local schools to promote physical education and healthy lifestyles.
