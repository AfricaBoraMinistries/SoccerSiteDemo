David Kimani

Biography

David Kimani is one of the most accomplished coaches in East African youth soccer. A former professional player with extensive international experience, he brings elite-level knowledge and connections to Africa Bora Soccer Academy.

Since joining the academy in 2020 as Technical Director and U18 Head Coach, David has transformed the program into a pathway for talented players to reach collegiate and professional levels. His network and reputation have opened doors for numerous academy graduates.

Coaching Experience

  • 2020-Present: Technical Director & U18 Head Coach, Africa Bora Soccer Academy
  • 2018-2020: Assistant Coach, National Youth Team (U20)
  • 2017-2018: Youth Academy Director, Gor Mahia FC
  • 2016-2017: Player Development Coach, Tusker FC

Playing Background

David enjoyed a distinguished 12-year professional career playing in Kenya, Tanzania, and Ethiopia. He was a versatile midfielder known for his tactical intelligence and leadership qualities.

Career Highlights:

  • Played for Gor Mahia FC (2004-2009), winning three league titles
  • Captained club to CAF Champions League quarter-finals (2008)
  • Represented Kenya National Team in 18 international matches
  • Named to Kenyan Premier League All-Star team (2007, 2008)
  • Played professionally in Tanzania for Simba SC (2010-2013)
  • Finished career with Ethiopian club St. George SC (2014-2016)

Certifications & Education

  • CAF A Coaching License (2017)
  • Advanced Coaching Diploma (2019)
  • UEFA B License (2020)
  • Player Scouting & Evaluation Certificate (2018)
  • Sports Management Certificate (2021)
  • First Aid & CPR Certified (2025)

Coaching Philosophy

"At the U18 level, we're preparing players for their next step, whether that's collegiate, professional, or simply playing at a high amateur level. My job is to give them the technical skills, tactical understanding, and mental preparation they need to succeed. But beyond soccer, I want to develop leaders who will make positive impacts in their communities."

David runs a demanding program that mirrors professional standards. He expects commitment, discipline, and continuous improvement from his players. However, he also emphasizes the importance of education and character development, ensuring his players are prepared for success in all areas of life.

Achievements as Coach

  • Led U18 team to Regional Championship and National Semi-Finals (2025)
  • Coached 12 players who earned college scholarships
  • Developed 4 players who signed professional contracts
  • Had 2 players selected for National Youth Team training camps
  • Named "Youth Development Coach of the Year" by National Soccer Association (2024)

Impact Beyond Coaching

As Technical Director, David oversees coaching development across all age groups at the academy. He conducts regular training sessions for coaches, ensuring consistent quality and methodology throughout the program. He also maintains relationships with scouts and coaches at universities and professional clubs, helping connect talented players with opportunities.

David is a sought-after speaker at coaching clinics and youth development conferences across East Africa, sharing his knowledge and passion for developing young talent.
