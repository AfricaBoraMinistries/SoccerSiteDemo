Our Teams

Africa Bora Soccer Academy fields teams across multiple age groups. Each team is carefully coached to develop technical skills, tactical understanding, and teamwork while emphasizing character development.

Under-12 Team

Age Group: 10-12 years

Our U12 team focuses on fundamental skills development, including dribbling, passing, and ball control. Players learn the basics of positioning and teamwork in a fun, supportive environment.

Coach: James Mwangi

Training: Tuesday & Thursday, 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Under-15 Team

Age Group: 13-15 years

The U15 team works on advanced technical skills and tactical awareness. Players develop their understanding of formations, set pieces, and game strategies while competing in regional tournaments.

Coach: Sarah Omondi

Training: Monday, Wednesday & Friday, 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Under-18 Team

Age Group: 16-18 years

Our senior team prepares players for potential opportunities at higher levels of competition. Training emphasizes physical conditioning, advanced tactics, and mental preparation. Several players have gone on to play at collegiate and professional levels.

Coach: David Kimani

Training: Monday through Friday, 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
